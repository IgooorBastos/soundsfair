## ğŸ” SEO Strategy for Crypto/Bitcoin Education (2025 Standards)

### Critical Requirements

#### 1. **E-E-A-T Framework** (Google's Quality Standard)
- **Experience:** Demonstrate real Bitcoin usage/knowledge
- **Expertise:** Author credibility, credentials, background
- **Authoritativeness:** Industry recognition, citations, backlinks
- **Trustworthiness:** HTTPS, privacy policy, risk disclaimers

**Action Items:**
- Add author bios with credentials
- Create "About the Team" page with real photos
- Add citation/source links to all educational content
- Implement risk disclaimers on all financial information
- Highlight years of experience, certifications

#### 2. **YMYL (Your Money Your Life) Compliance**
Bitcoin content falls under YMYL, requiring:
- Extreme accuracy in all claims
- Regular content audits and updates
- Clear distinction: educational vs. financial advice
- Medical-level fact-checking standards

**Action Items:**
- Add "Last Updated" dates to all content
- Implement content review workflow
- Add disclaimer: "Educational purposes only, not financial advice"
- Create editorial standards document

#### 3. **Content Types That Rank Well**
âœ… **High-performing formats:**
- Beginner's guides ("What is Bitcoin for complete beginners")
- Step-by-step tutorials ("How to buy Bitcoin safely in 2025")
- Comprehensive FAQs (voice search optimization)
- Case studies and success stories
- Thought leadership articles
- Video content with transcripts
- Glossaries and knowledge bases

âŒ **Avoid:**
- Shallow, keyword-stuffed content
- Outdated information
- Unverified claims
- Missing sources/citations

#### 4. **Technical SEO Essentials**

- XML sitemap (needs update with new pages)
- Breadcrumb navigation

#### 5. **Video Content Integration**
