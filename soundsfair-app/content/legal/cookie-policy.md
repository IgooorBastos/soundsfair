## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device and store certain information about your preferences or actions.

### Types of Cookies:

**Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser.

**Persistent Cookies:** Cookies that remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them.

**First-Party Cookies:** Set by the website you're visiting (soundsfair.com).

**Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services we use (e.g., analytics providers, payment processors).

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

### 1. Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the Website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:

- **Authentication:** Remembering your login status (if logged in)
- **Security:** Protecting against unauthorized access and CSRF attacks
- **Session Management:** Maintaining your session as you navigate the site
- **Progress Tracking:** Saving your course progress and quiz results

**You cannot opt out of essential cookies** as the Website will not function properly without them.

### 2. Analytics Cookies (Performance)

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors interact with our Website. This helps us improve user experience and content.

**Services we may use:**
- Google Analytics (or privacy-focused alternatives like Plausible Analytics)
- Vercel Analytics (website performance monitoring)

**Information collected:**
- Pages visited and time spent
- Navigation patterns and user flows
- Device type, browser, and screen resolution
- Geographic location (country/city level, not precise location)
- Referring websites (how you found us)

**Note:** We use analytics tools in privacy-respecting modes when possible (e.g., anonymized IP addresses).

### 3. Functional Cookies (Preferences)

These cookies remember your preferences and choices to provide a personalized experience:

- **Theme Preferences:** Light/dark mode settings
- **Language Preferences:** Selected language (when multi-language is available)
- **Notification Preferences:** Dismissed notifications or announcements
- **Tool Settings:** Saved calculator inputs or preferences

**You can opt out of functional cookies**, but some features may not work as expected.

### 4. Marketing Cookies (Optional)

We may use marketing cookies to show relevant content and advertisements:

- **Social Media Cookies:** From embedded content (YouTube videos, Twitter embeds)
- **Affiliate Tracking:** To track referrals from affiliate links
- **Newsletter Tracking:** To understand which content drives subscriptions

**You can opt out of marketing cookies** through your browser settings or cookie consent banner.

## How to Manage Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

### 1. Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to:
- View and delete cookies
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies only
- Receive notifications when cookies are set

**How to access cookie settings:**

**Google Chrome:** Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

**Firefox:** Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

**Safari:** Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

**Edge:** Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

**Note:** Blocking all cookies may prevent the Website from functioning properly.

### 2. Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Website, you'll see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Reject non-essential cookies
- Customize cookie preferences

You can change your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

### 3. Analytics Opt-Out

To opt out of Google Analytics specifically:
- Install the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### 4. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect Do Not Track (DNT) browser signals. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will not track your activity for analytics purposes.

**Note:** DNT support varies across browsers. We make best efforts to honor it when technically possible.
