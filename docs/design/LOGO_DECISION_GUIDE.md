We'll evaluate each logo across 10 critical dimensions:

1. **Brand Alignment** - How well does it represent "fair money education"?

8. **Recognition Speed** - How quickly can people identify it?
9. **Implementation Ease** - How easy is it to create and use?
10. **Growth Potential** - Can it evolve with the brand?
