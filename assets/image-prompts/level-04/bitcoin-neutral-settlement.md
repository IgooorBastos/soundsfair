## Primary AI Generation Prompt

Create a conceptual diagram showing Bitcoin as a neutral settlement layer between nations, displayed on a pure black background (#000000). Show a horizontal composition with multiple simplified nation/economy icons arranged in a circle or semi-circle around the perimeter: represent 5-6 different regions using abstract geometric shapes or simplified flag colors (USA, China, EU, Russia, Middle East, Global South) in their respective representative colors (muted versions). In the center, place a large Bitcoin symbol (₿) in bright libertarian gold (#FFD000) as the neutral hub. From each nation/region, show bidirectional arrows (white) connecting to the central Bitcoin hub, representing cross-border settlement flows. The arrows should be equal in size and prominence - no nation has preferential access or control. Surrounding the Bitcoin symbol, add a subtle circular boundary or ring suggesting neutrality, perhaps with text around it reading "No Single Nation Controls" or "Permissionless Global Access" in small white text. The key visual concept is that Bitcoin sits outside and above geopolitical conflicts - it's a neutral protocol that any nation can use without requiring permission from rivals. Below the main diagram, include a comparison table or simple visual: "Traditional Settlement: Swift/CBDC → Controlled by US/China" (red) vs "Bitcoin Settlement: Neutral Protocol → Controlled by Math" (gold). Style should be geopolitical analysis infographic mixed with protocol diagram - technical but accessible. No photographs, no realistic imagery, clean geometric design. The composition should convey neutrality, equality of access, and freedom from geopolitical capture.

## Alternative Prompt Variations

### Variation A (Sanctions Resistance):
Show traditional payment rails (Swift) blocked by barriers/walls (representing sanctions in red), contrasted with Bitcoin flowing freely around obstacles. Emphasizes censorship resistance. Two-panel side-by-side comparison.

### Variation B (No Man's Land):
Bitcoin represented as neutral territory (like Switzerland) sitting between geopolitical power blocs. Visual metaphor of a neutral zone. Gold safe zone in center, conflicting nations around perimeter.

### Variation C (Protocol vs Politics):
Abstract visualization showing Bitcoin as mathematical rules (represented by equations, code, or geometric patterns in gold) immune to political pressure (represented by flags, government symbols fading away or blocked). Emphasizes "code over politics."
