# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 2024

## Introduction

Welcome to SoundsFair ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website soundsfair.com (the "Site").

**By using our Site, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.**

---

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

We may collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

- **Email address** (if you subscribe to our newsletter or use the Ask feature)
- **Payment information** (Lightning Network invoice data for paid features)
- **Questions submitted** (via Ask page)
- **Survey responses** (if you participate in feedback forms)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information:

- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, time spent, links clicked
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device type
- **Analytics Data:** Via Plausible Analytics (privacy-focused, no cookies, GDPR compliant)
- **IP Address:** Anonymized for security and analytics purposes

### 3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

- **Essential Cookies:** Required for site functionality (e.g., remembering your progress in Learn Path)
- **Analytics:** We use Plausible Analytics, which does NOT use cookies and is GDPR compliant
- **No Third-Party Tracking:** We do NOT use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or other invasive trackers

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

1. **Providing Services:** Deliver educational content, DCA calculator, Learn Path
2. **Communication:** Send responses to paid Q&A, newsletter updates (opt-in only)
3. **Improvement:** Analyze usage patterns to improve content and features
4. **Security:** Prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
5. **Compliance:** Meet legal obligations (tax reporting for Lightning payments)

**We do NOT:**
- ❌ Sell your personal information
- ❌ Share your data with advertisers
- ❌ Track you across other websites
- ❌ Use your data for targeted advertising

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, our legal basis for processing your data is:

- **Consent:** You provide explicit consent (e.g., newsletter signup)
